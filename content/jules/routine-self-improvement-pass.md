# Routine: Self-Improvement Pass

**Goal:** Read the "Run Log" section in `content/jules/index.md`, analyze feedback from previous routine executions, apply improvements to the respective routine files, and archive the processed logs.

## Task Details
1. **Read the Logs:** Open `content/jules/index.md` and read all entries in the "Run Log" section.
2. **Analyze Feedback:** Identify any notes, learnings, or suggestions left by previous runs of routines.
3. **Improve Routines:** Open the `.md` file for each routine that left constructive feedback. Update the `Task Details` or `Execution Reminders` section of that routine to incorporate the learnings and make the routine more effective or less error-prone for future runs.
4. **Archive Logs:** Once a log entry has been processed (either its feedback was incorporated, or it contained no actionable feedback), remove it from the "Run Log" section in `content/jules/index.md`. You may optionally move them to an archive file like `content/jules/run-log-archive.md` if you wish, but the primary index must be cleared.

## Execution Reminders
* Do not change the fundamental goal of the routines you are modifying. Only refine their instructions based on empirical evidence from the logs.
* Make sure `content/jules/index.md` remains clean and valid markdown after removing the processed logs.
* **Upon successful completion, you MUST append a short log (including date, routine name, success status, and a summary of how many routines you improved) to the "Run Log" section of `content/jules/index.md`.**
