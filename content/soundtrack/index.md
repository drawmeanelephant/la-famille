# Soundtrack

This folder contains the track listings and engineering details for the project's soundtrack albums.

## Vision

The soundtrack embodies the process of working on the project and will consist of a few different albums:

1.  **Nerd Core Boom Bap:** A boom bap explainer style album, leaning into nerd core.
2.  **Touring Funk/Bluegrass:** A touring funk or bluegrass kind of style.
3.  **French Chanson / Comedic Jazz:** A cabaret-style album exploring the AI collaboration and the pun origin of the project name (The Family "Jules").

These albums will document the journey and hopefully be pretty good tracks!
