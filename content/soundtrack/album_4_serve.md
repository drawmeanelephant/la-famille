# Volume 4: Serving Bytes

This album is dedicated to the addition of the local development server and terminal takeover to La Famille.

1. **"Full Screen Buffer"** - A driving synthwave track with deep bass drops representing `tea.WithAltScreen()` clearing away the noise and taking full control.
2. **"Localhost 8080"** - Upbeat, steady house beat, simulating the reliable rhythm of a local development server successfully serving static assets.
3. **"Dance of the Octopus"** - A whimsical, slightly chaotic chiptune track inspired by Raoul dancing while the site is being served in the background.
4. **"Ctrl+C to Stop"** - A mellow, fading out chillhop track representing a graceful `server.Shutdown()` when escaping the serve screen.
