# "Sentinel's Watch"

**Prompt for Flow Music:**
Create a driving, atmospheric synthwave track with a strong, steady bassline and precise, calculating arpeggios. The mood should feel vigilant, secure, and methodical, like an automated guardian sweeping through code late at night. Use a deep, resonant kick drum and sharp, clean claps or snares. The track should feel structured and unyielding, reflecting the rigorous process of finding and fixing vulnerabilities. Add subtle, high-pitched tech "ping" sound effects occasionally to represent successfully identifying an issue.
