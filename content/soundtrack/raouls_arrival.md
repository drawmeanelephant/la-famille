# Raoul(s)'s Arrival

**Genre:** Synthwave / French Electro
**Vibe:** Upbeat, modern, welcoming
**Prompt:** A punchy, synthwave track with a French electro influence. Driving bassline, shimmering synthesizers, and a welcoming, triumphant melody that introduces Raoul(s), the new eight-tentacled mascot of La Famille. It feels like unlocking a new character in a retro-futuristic arcade game.

*Added to celebrate the naming of our mascot, Raoul(s), and the integration of new graphics across our brutalist, centered, split-screen, and luxury magazine themes.*
