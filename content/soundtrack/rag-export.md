# The Bundle Ritual

**Prompt:** A focused, pulsing electronic track with deep bass drops and precise, high-tech synthesizers. The beat should feel methodical and organized, like data being gathered and compressed. The melody is clean and bright, representing clarity and structure. A vocal sample repeats "Collect, Compress, Context."

**Explanation:** This track was created to celebrate the addition of the `rag` command to the `la-famille` CLI. This powerful new tool allows us to bundle the entire project—system code, configurations, templates, and content—into three RAG-friendly markdown files. It's a ritual of organization, preparing our codebase to be perfectly understood by other AI minds like Gemini or NotebookLM.
