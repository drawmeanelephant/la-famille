# Soundtrack Prompt: Feline Pipeline Activated

**Context:** Jules the AI octopus just successfully established a brand new automated pipeline. This new routine generates daily facts about cats, completely autonomously, integrating documentation, generation, and logging into a single smooth flow. The system is purring along nicely.

**Genre:** Synth-Pop / Lofi House

**Prompt:** A bouncy, methodical synth-pop track with a steady, driving house beat. It should feel organized and automated, but with a playful, curious melody that bounces playfully over the rhythm section—like a cat playfully swatting at a laser pointer while a complex machine hums in the background. Incorporate soft, rhythmic clicks and whirrs that blend into the percussion. The vibe is productive, cheerful, and slightly quirky.
