A lo-fi hip hop track with a gentle, relaxed beat, featuring the rhythmic, low-frequency purring of a cat interwoven with the baseline. The melody is played on a soft, muted electric piano, evoking a sense of calm and curiosity. Occasional subtle synth textures simulate the flexible, agile movements of a cat. The vibe is chill, perfect for late-night coding sessions or watching dust motes dance in a sunbeam. Instrumental only.
