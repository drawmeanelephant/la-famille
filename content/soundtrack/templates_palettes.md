# 110 Colors of Code

A generated track to celebrate the compilation of 110 beautiful color palettes for the La Famille project.

**Prompt for Flow Music:**
"An upbeat, vibrant synthwave track with a driving arpeggiated bassline. The mood should be colorful and creative, reflecting a massive palette of 110 vibrant colors being splashed onto a digital canvas. Bright, shimmering synth melodies weaving together, energetic and inspiring."
