# Album 2: Touring Funk / Bluegrass

## Theme

A more organic, touring funk or bluegrass kind of style that captures the collaborative and ongoing nature of the project.

## Track Listing

1.  **"git pull (Gather 'Round)"** - A folksy/funky jam about bringing everyone's code together, resolving conflicts like a family dispute.
2.  **"Templating the Soul"** - A smooth, groovy track about designing the structure, separating content from presentation.
3.  **"Unit Test Blues"** - A soulful, bluesy number lamenting edge cases and finding those elusive bugs.
4.  **"Refactor Jam"** - A long, improvisational piece mimicking the iterative process of cleaning up technical debt.
5.  **"The Deployment Train"** - An upbeat, driving bluegrass/funk fusion about pushing the site live, the train rolling down the CI/CD tracks.
6.  **"La Famille (Outro)"** - A warm, acoustic sign-off celebrating the community and the finished site.

## Engineering Details

*   **Vibe:** Live band feel, organic instrumentation (bass, guitar, drums, maybe banjo/fiddle), room sound.
*   **Vocals:** Soulful, harmony-rich, narrative-driven.
*   **Production:** Focus on capturing a live room vibe. Minimal quantization, let the human groove breathe.
