# Track: Trim and Tidy

## Flow Music Prompt

**Style:** Minimalist Synth-Pop, clean and precise

**Prompt:**
Create a minimalist synth-pop track with a clean, precise, and rhythmic character. The sound should be clinical yet pleasant, representing the act of refactoring and simplifying code. Use sharp, short synth stabs, a driving but understated four-on-the-floor kick, and a repetitive melodic sequence that feels like clockwork. The texture should be thin and transparent, with lots of space between the notes. Incorporate subtle "click" and "snip" sound effects to symbolize the trimming of suffixes. The mood is one of quiet efficiency and the satisfaction of a job well done. The tempo should be around 110 BPM.
