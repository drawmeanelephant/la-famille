# Cobra Strike (The CLI Anthem)

*Genre: Synthwave / Cyberpunk Electro*

**Prompt for Flow Music:**
> A driving, high-energy synthwave track with a pulsating bassline and retro-futuristic arpeggios. It builds steadily, representing the structured power of a command-line interface. The drop is sharp and precise, like executing a well-crafted subcommand. "Go run build," the beat seems to say, "content dir to out."

**Lore:**
As the La Famille generator grew, the old standard `flag` package began to feel like a tangled web of cables. The engineers needed a weapon, a tool of precision. They reached for the Cobra. With a swift strike, the monolithic `main.go` was sliced into a root command and a sleek `build` subcommand. Flags became predictable (`--contentDir`, `--out`), and the help menus bloomed with clarity. The CLI was no longer just a script; it was an interface, ready to scale, ready to serve the family.
