# Track: The Cleanup Sweep

## Flow Music Prompt

**Style:** Lo-fi hip hop, chillhop, relaxing study beats

**Prompt:**
Create a chill, lo-fi hip hop beat with a relaxing, mellow vibe. It should sound like a productive afternoon of getting organized. The instrumentation should feature a smooth, slightly detuned electric piano, a deep sub-bass line, and a crisp but dusty drum break. Sprinkle in some subtle sound design elements like the sound of rustling papers, a broom sweeping gently, or old vinyl crackle. The mood is satisfying, tidy, and focused, celebrating the feeling of clearing out old tasks and neatly organizing the project's music folder. The tempo should be around 75 BPM, with a head-nodding groove that helps you concentrate.
