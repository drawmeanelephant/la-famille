# Flow Music Prompt: The Great Cleanup

**Style:** Synth-pop / Upbeat Lo-Fi

**Prompt:** An upbeat, productive lo-fi synth-pop track about organizing files, sweeping up the digital debris, and consolidating folders. The music should feel bouncy and methodical, like a time-lapse of someone cleaning a messy room and putting everything exactly where it belongs. Minimalist electronic beats with satisfying, crisp percussive clicks and snaps.

**Lyrics:**
(Verse 1)
Sweep the files into the trash,
Test results get a brand new stash.
Moving data to the assets bin,
A tidy repo where we can win.

(Chorus)
Capital J or lowercase,
We put 'em all in a single space.
Scrubbing down the root, yeah we're keeping it clean,
The sharpest looking repo that you've ever seen.
