# Brutalist Beats

**Task:** Create a Brutalist UI template for La Famille.
**Vibe:** Raw, Industrial, Bold, High-Contrast, No-Nonsense.

## Flow Music Prompt
> A high-energy industrial techno track with heavy, distorted kicks and sharp, metallic percussion. Incorporate rhythmic white noise and glitchy, repetitive synth loops. The sound should feel raw and uncompromising, mirroring a brutalist architectural aesthetic. Fast-paced, intense, and strictly monochrome in its sonic palette.

## Reflection
The Brutalist UI demands a soundtrack that doesn't apologize for its presence. It's about structure, function, and the beauty of the raw material. This track provides the sonic foundation for building with uncompromising precision.
