# 5 Fascinating Feline Facts

1. **A Cat's Purr Can Heal:** Domestic cats purr at a frequency between 25 and 150 Hertz. This frequency range has been shown in studies to improve bone density and promote tissue regeneration, meaning their purr is a built-in healing mechanism.
2. **Noses Like Fingerprints:** Just as human fingerprints are unique, the pattern of ridges and bumps on a cat's nose pad is completely unique to that individual cat. No two cats have the exact same nose print.
3. **Masters of Sleep:** Cats spend an impressive 70% of their lives sleeping. That means a nine-year-old cat has only been awake for roughly three years of its life!
4. **Not Just for Balance:** A cat's tail contains almost 10% of all the bones in its body. It's not just a crucial counterweight for balance during acrobatic feats, but also a complex communicative tool.
5. **Taste Bud Deficiency:** Unlike dogs or humans, cats are genetically unable to taste sweetness. They lack the specific taste receptor necessary to perceive sweet flavors, which explains why they are true obligate carnivores.
