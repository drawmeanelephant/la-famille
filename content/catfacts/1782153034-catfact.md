# 5 Fascinating Facts About Felines

1. **They have a unique walk:** Cats walk like camels and giraffes: They move both of their right feet first, then move both of their left feet. No other animals walk this way.
2. **Purring frequency:** A cat's purr vibrates at a frequency of 25 to 150 hertz, which is the same frequency at which muscles and bones repair themselves.
3. **Nose prints:** Much like human fingerprints, the pattern of ridges and bumps on a cat's nose pad is unique to each individual cat.
4. **Sweet blindness:** Cats are completely unable to taste sweetness due to a genetic mutation that disabled the gene responsible for the sweet taste receptor.
5. **Flexible spines:** A cat can jump up to six times its height because of the powerful muscles in its back legs and a flexible spine consisting of 53 loosely fitting vertebrae (humans only have 34).
