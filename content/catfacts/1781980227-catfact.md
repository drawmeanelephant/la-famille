# 5 Interesting Cat Facts

1. **A group of cats is called a clowder.** A male cat is called a tom, a female cat is called a molly or queen, and young cats are called kittens.
2. **Cats spend a large portion of their lives sleeping.** In fact, they sleep for around 13 to 16 hours a day (or 70% of their life).
3. **Cats have an extra organ that allows them to taste scents.** It's called the Jacobson's organ, and it's located in the roof of their mouth.
4. **Cats can jump up to six times their height.** Their powerful leg muscles allow them to achieve impressive feats of agility.
5. **The oldest known pet cat was found in a 9,500-year-old grave.** This discovery was made on the Mediterranean island of Cyprus.
