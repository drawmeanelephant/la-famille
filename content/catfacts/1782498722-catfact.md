1. **Felicette, the Space Cat:** In 1963, a French stray cat named Félicette became the first and only cat to travel to space and survive, experiencing five minutes of weightlessness.
2. **Mayor Stubbs:** A ginger tabby named Stubbs served as the honorary mayor of Talkeetna, Alaska, for 20 years from 1997 until his passing in 2017.
3. **Stationmaster Tama:** Tama, a calico cat, saved the Wakayama Electric Railway in Japan from bankruptcy by drawing thousands of tourists in her official role as stationmaster.
4. **Towser the Mouser:** A long-haired tortoiseshell named Towser holds the Guinness World Record for the most mice caught, estimated at 28,899 during her lifetime at the Glenturret Distillery in Scotland.
5. **Ta-Miu:** The ancient Egyptian crown prince Thutmose, brother of Akhenaten, had a beloved pet cat named Ta-Miu, whose intricately carved limestone sarcophagus can still be seen today.
