# 5 Interesting Cat Facts

1. **Purring Frequency:** Domestic cats purr at a frequency between 25 and 150 Hertz, which happens to be the frequency at which muscles and bones best grow and repair themselves.
2. **Nose Prints:** Much like human fingerprints, a cat's nose pad has a unique pattern of ridges and bumps, meaning no two cats have the exact same nose print.
3. **Collarbone-less:** Cats do not have a collarbone (clavicle) that connects to other bones; instead, it is buried within the shoulder muscle. This allows them to fit through almost any space their head can get through.
4. **Taste Receptors:** Felines are one of the few mammals that lack the taste receptors for sweetness. A genetic mutation means they cannot taste sugars.
5. **Sleeping Hours:** Cats typically sleep for 12 to 16 hours a day, meaning a 9-year-old cat has been awake for only about 3 years of its life.
